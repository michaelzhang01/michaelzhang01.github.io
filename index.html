Michael Zhang

Michael Zhang is currently a PhD student at the University of Texas at Austin in the Department of Statistics and Data Science. His research interests are statistical machine learning and natural language processing. Prior to his graduate studies, Michael was an analyst at Rule14 LLC. in Santa Monica, California. Michael Zhang earned a B.S. in Statistics, a B.A in Political Science and a Minor in Russian at the University of California, Santa Barbara.
